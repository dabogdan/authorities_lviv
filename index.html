About the Project

`Who can help me?` `Where to find services, remedy and recourse?` These are questions which can become pertinent to everyone. Various institutions in your area are there for you stand ready to provide assistance and advice. However, often times it is not clear where to find them and to know what they can do. Addressing the right institution in the right place does not only save time for the citizen and the institution, it also renders a more tangible result.

This site is a common effort of the participating institutions and EUAM. It strives to bring the local and regional institutions closer to all. It allows for a better orientation, since it does not only show the exact location and contact, but also explains what institutions do and what they do not do. With that, the user is enabled to have a quicker, closer, and better-informed access.
